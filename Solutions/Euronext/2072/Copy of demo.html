Companies listed on Euronext are indexed according to size, segments, sectors and per national market. It is not necessary to apply for inclusion in an index, just as a company cannot block its inclusion.

Dematerialisation notices intro

The  dematerialisation process in Belgium obliges the issuers of physical securities to sell any non-reconciled positions they may have in these securities.

Euronext Expert Market facilitates the sale of non-listed securities by public auction.

Issuers wishing to dematerialise their securities can download the Standard Notice for dematerialisation:

  • Template for issuer publication in the context of the dematerialisation (article 11, Law on dematerialisation) French
  • Template for issuer publication in the context of the dematerialisation (article 11, Law on dematerialisation)  Dutch. 

For more information regarding dematerialisation, please visit www.dmat.be.
